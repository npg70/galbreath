***Orsla Galbreath***, daughter of [*James Galbreath*](galbreath-james-abt-1775.md) and *Martha Galbreath*, baptized at Campbeltown 24 Nov 1805.  Married at Campbeltown 03 Feb 1829 to **Hugh Templeton**.
